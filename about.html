Two Decades of Purpose

From a small community initiative to an international force for change — our story is written in the lives we've touched.

Our Story

Founded on a Simple Belief

In 2005, a group of community leaders came together with a shared conviction: that sustainable change begins from within communities themselves.

What began as a single healthcare initiative in rural Kenya has grown into an international organization operating across 18 countries, touching over 250,000 lives.

Today, OEVI is recognized as a leading voice in community-led development — partnering with governments, foundations, and local leaders to build a more equitable future.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

Key moments in our two-decade journey of impact.

2005

The Beginning

Founded in Nairobi with our first mobile healthcare clinic serving 3 villages in rural Kenya.

2010

Expanding Horizons

Launched education and agriculture programs across 4 countries, reaching 10,000 beneficiaries.

2015

100,000 Lives

Reached our first major milestone — 100,000 direct beneficiaries across East Africa.

2020

Global Recognition

Awarded UN Partnership status and expanded operations to 12 countries across Sub-Saharan Africa.

2026

Today

Operating in 18 countries with 120+ active projects and 250,000+ lives transformed.

What Guides Us

Mission, Vision & Values

The principles that drive everything we do.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable communities through sustainable programs in health, education, agriculture, and social justice — creating self-reliant futures.

Our Vision

A world where every individual — regardless of background — has access to opportunity, dignity, and the chance to thrive.

Core Values

Integrity, compassion, transparency, community ownership, and sustainable impact guide every decision we make.
